Today I've released the first version of [**Tweesten**](https://github.com/errebenito/tweesten), a Twitter bot written in Python that tweets a collage of album covers based on your last.fm scrobbles. It requires tweepy 3.5.0 or newer and is fully customizable through an ini file. You can grab the latest release [**here**](https://github.com/errebenito/tweesten/releases/latest).
